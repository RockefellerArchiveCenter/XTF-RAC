Rockefeller Archive Center Fee Schedule

PDFs

Duplication requests for PDFs are limited to 500 pages per order.

  • Unit cost: $0.50 per page
  • Service charge: $25.00 per order

Shipping

  • Electronic Delivery: free
  • USPS (Domestic): $6.00
  • USPS (Mexico/Canada): variable cost
  • FedEx (International): variable cost

Photocopies

Duplication requests for photocopies are limited to 500 pages per order.

  • Unit cost: $0.50 per page
  • Service charge: $25.00 per order

Shipping

  • USPS (Domestic): $6.00
  • USPS (Mexico/Canada): variable cost
  • FedEx (International): variable cost

Scans (TIFF and JPEG)

  • Unit cost: $25.00 per image
  • Service charge: $25.00 per order

Shipping

  • Electronic Delivery: free
  • USPS (Domestic): $6.00
  • USPS (Mexico/Canada): $12.00
  • FedEx (International): $14.00

Scans (TIFF and JPEG)

If DVD access copies of moving image materials exist, the following fee schedule applies. If access copies do not exist, the fee schedule under “Outsourced Duplication” applies.

  • Unit cost: $15.00 per title (usually one title per DVD)
  • Service charge: $25.00 per order

Shipping (up to 20 DVDs)

  • USPS (Domestic): $6.00
  • USPS (Mexico/Canada): $12.00
  • FedEx (International): $14.00

Scans (TIFF and JPEG)

Duplication of any material that is larger than 11 by 17 inches, bound, three-dimensional, fragile or damaged, as well as duplication of audio-visual materials, will be outsourced to an external vendor. Please contact an archivist at archive@rockarch.org for further details including estimates for time and cost.
